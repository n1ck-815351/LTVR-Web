LTVR Administrator Page

Welcome to the Link Manager! This tool is designed to assist organization owners in generating links for their respective organizations. Here's an overview of the user roles and their permissions within the system:

  • Administrators: As an administrator, you have full control over your organization. You can manage schools, create and manage accounts for other users, and customize various settings.
  • Moderators: Moderators have limited administrative privileges. They can manage accounts with lower permission levels within their organization, making them ideal for assisting administrators with day-to-day tasks.
  • Teachers: Teachers have the ability to create, edit, and delete classes within their assigned schools or organizations. They can also create student accounts specifically designed for virtual reality experiences.
  • Students: Students can access their schedule and view information about their school and organization. They can explore virtual reality content and participate in immersive learning experiences.

Users can be assigned multiple roles to accommodate various responsibilities. For example, you can assign a user as both a Moderator and a Teacher to grant them additional privileges.
